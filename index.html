Hello, my name is Pedro Villa. I have always had a passion for business, marketing, and networking, whether it be for myself or an organization. Growing up in New York, I believe my business mindset was shaped by my surroundings. As a child, I had many aspirations, but my creativity has always been a driving force. Currently, I am majoring in Interactive Digital Media and Marketing.
I find my true calling in this field, where I have the creative freedom to bring others' visions and dreams to life. I have proficiency in Adobe Suites, professional communications, public speaking, and Digital Video/effects. As a highly motivated student, I am a quick learner and approach everything I do with passion and an open mind.
I am seeking an opportunity to utilize my experience, skills, and exceptional work ethic to succeed in my future endeavors. I am looking for an organization that values dedication and delivers results. Ultimately, my dream is to own a PR and Marketing firm. While PR is a subset of marketing, both disciplines focus on building relationships with the public, which is crucial for successful product marketing. In the end, there are only two options: innovate or market it.
